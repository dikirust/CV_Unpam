PROJECT RESULTS (SUMMARY)
================================================================================

DATASET:
- Total Images: 8,400
- Classes: 5 (Foodwaste, Glass, Metal, Paper, Plastic)
- Split: 70% train (5,880), 15% validation (1,260), 15% test (1,260)

MODEL COMPARISON:

Custom CNN:
  - Architecture: 3 Conv blocks (32→64→128 filters)
  - Parameters: 310,405
  - Accuracy: 90.16%
  - Precision: 90.23%
  - Recall: 90.21%
  - F1-Score: 90.21%
  - Training Time: 451 seconds (~7.5 min)

MobileNetV2:
  - Architecture: Transfer learning from ImageNet
  - Parameters: 87.5M (base) + custom head
  - Accuracy: 93.65% ★ WINNER
  - Precision: 93.68%
  - Recall: 93.72%
  - F1-Score: 93.70%
  - Training Time: 231 seconds (~3.8 min)

KEY FINDING:
MobileNetV2 outperforms Custom CNN by 3.49% in accuracy and trains 2x faster,
demonstrating the effectiveness of transfer learning for this task.
